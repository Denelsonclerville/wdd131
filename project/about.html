Our Mission

At Economax, we strive to provide local Haitian products to everyone while supporting artisans and local producers. We focus on quality, affordability, and creating a seamless shopping experience.

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Economax began as a small initiative to connect buyers with authentic Haitian goods. Since then, we have expanded to include hundreds of products ranging from artisan crafts to food items.

Our Values

Supporting local Haitian artisans and producers.

Providing affordable,high-quality products.

Ensuring an easy and safe online shopping experience.

Encouraging sustainable consumption and smart shopping.
